## 👋 About Me

**Computer Vision Software Engineer** with 4+ years of experience developing high-performance **C++ machine vision** systems for industrial inspection. Specialized in **pattern recognition, high-density gauging, and real-time algorithm optimization**, with a strong foundation in **mathematics**, **statistics**, **linear algebra**, and **data analysis**.

Developed multiple computer vision projects including **object detection**, **metrology**, and **automation systems**, with deployment experience in semiconductor production environments. Experienced in benchmarking and optimizing algorithms against industry standards while maintaining high accuracy and reliability in real-world production systems.
